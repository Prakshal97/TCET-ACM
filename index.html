About ACM TCET

A global ACM network, rooted at TCET.

ACM is the world’s largest computing society. At TCET, our student chapter gives learners a place to collaborate, build, experiment and grow alongside peers and mentors.

Established in 2001, ACM TCET empowers students to learn beyond the curriculum, share knowledge and discover real opportunities across computing disciplines.

Events & Flagship Drives

Experiences that go beyond classrooms.

Seminars, hackathons, alumni meets, industrial visits and social initiatives that let students explore how technology and community-building work in the real world.

A year full of touchpoints.

With more than a dozen activities spread across the academic year, ACM TCET ensures that students get multiple chances to explore, build and lead.

ACM TCET Annual Flow

• Sem 1 — Onboarding, alumni interactions, hackathons & core activities.
• Mid-year — Thematic seminars, industrial visits & hands-on sprints.
• Sem 2 — Advanced workshops, chapter showcases & collaborations.
• All year — Student-led initiatives, SIGs and peer-learning circles.
